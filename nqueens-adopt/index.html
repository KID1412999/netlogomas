NQueens Adopt

WHAT IS IT?

This program solves the n-queens problem using the Adopt algorith.
The Adopt algorithm appears in

  • Pragnesh Jay Modi, Wei-Min Shen, Milind Tambe, and Makoto Yokoo. An Asynchronous Complete Method for General Distributed Constraint Optimization. In Proceedings of Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent Systems Workshop on Distributed Constraint Reasoning, p. 104--118, 2002.
